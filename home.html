Personas and Storyboarding

Overview

The process of observing users interacting with an interface, interviewing these users, creating personas based on these users, and creating a storyboard based on a persona is a classic UX task. As a student of CS1300 (UI/UX), our first assignment was to experience this entire process to better understand what UI/UX designers do.

Unfortunately, COVID-19 has made it a bit more difficult to observe public interfaces. As a compromise, I chose my appartment's washing machine because I think it has a relatively complex and interesting interface. I then asked a couple of close friends to interact with my washing machine so that I could go through the same process.
